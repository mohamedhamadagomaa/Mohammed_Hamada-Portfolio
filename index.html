First contact with programming

My first exposure to programming was through studying. I entered the Faculty of Science at Zagazig University and succeeded in joining the Computer Science Department In 2015.

How did you start in web programming?

Fortunately for me, in the second year of college, we were asked to work on a web programming project. It was big for us at the time, but it was the beginning of everything.

Feeling passion for the field for the first time

During this period, none of my friends were passionate about the field, but my anxiety about the idea of ​​doing a project made me do a lot of research to understand what the field is and how to start such projects.

Love and passion for programming

The research made me master something that I later learned was the most important thing in programming, which is how to search for what I needed. However, although I encountered things other than the web during the research, it was what caught my attention the most.

My first project in web programming

I came up with the idea of ​​making a project about renting cars called AutoGear. It was the beginning, and thanks to God, I was able to accomplish every point in the project. It was my first explanation in this field in front of my teacher and my fellow students.

Final year and graduation project

In the last year of university, as usual, we were asked for a graduation project as a separate subject. I had delved more deeply into this field and was more comfortable working on it. It was about Restaurant Reviews and it's locations and we worked on it together with each other using various technologies.

Finishing university, joining the army as a reserve officer

Finishing university, joining the army as a reserve officer in the Air Defense Forces, and spending three years as an operations officer, during which I learned a lot of communication skills.

End of the army and continue learning and looking for a new suitable job

Here the army period has ended and a new phase of my life has begun, in which I hope I will have success. In the end, I can say do not worry as long as you continue to work and learn. Worry only when you stop doing so.
